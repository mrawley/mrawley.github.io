Built deliberately as a reusable capability, not a one-off — which is what leadership called out
by name. The bundle unlocks subscription-attach revenue tied to high-visibility title launches,
and coordinating the global GTA VI release meant working across eight-plus teams (pricing,
ingestion, gaming insights, risk/fraud, groups platform, marketing, and account management)
through a rolling launch that followed midnight across every time zone.
